THE UTILITARIAN PAPERCLIP

Paperclips are a practical product that anyone and everyone can use. From students to businessmen, hobbyists to professionals, paper clips are accessible and inclusive. So, it's through this neat little tool we've decided to promote another message of inclusivity.

“FINISH ANY PROJECT WITH PRIDE.”

GAYPERCLIPS are a novelty brand of paperclip we've curated in support of the LGBTQ+ community. These paperclips are colored in a rainbow variety to represent the estripes of the universal Pride flag. With sizes ranging from mini to jumbo, GAYPERCLIPS can help you finish any project with pride.

“OUR PRIDE ISN'T JUST FOR SHOW.”

At GAYPERCLIPS we believe in love, compassion, inclusivity, and equality. We have elected to do our part to support the LGBTQ+ cause in more ways than just providing fun, trendy stationary; because our pride isn't just for show. That's why 25% of all proceeds will benefit LGBTQ+ organizations including The Trevor Project, GLAAD, and OutRight Action International.

OUR STORY

Based in Boston, Massachusetts, the first state to legally acknowledge and approve same sex marriage, GAYPERCLIPS was established in 2008 with a motive for change. As lovers of stationary, we thought a rainbow set of paperclips would be an efficient and stylish way to stand for something bigger, while satisfying the need for paperclips everywhere. We're paperclips for a greater cause, and hope to cater to like-minded progressives who support independent business and gay rights.
